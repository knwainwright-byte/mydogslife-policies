Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

My Dog's Life is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Purchase History: When you buy credit packs, Apple processes the payment and shares transaction data with us.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app usage (crashes, performance) to improve the app.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Photos of your dog (not stored on our servers)
  • Location data
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Advertising identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

Photos you analyze are:

  • Temporarily uploaded to our secure servers for AI analysis
  • Deleted immediately after processing (within seconds)
  • Never stored, shared, or used for training

Thumbnails are stored locally on your device in an encrypted database (Core Data).

5. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Apple App Store: For payments and app distribution
  • OpenRouter AI: For dog behavior analysis (images deleted after processing)
  • Vercel: For backend hosting (no personal data stored)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data (contact us)
  • Opt out of analytics (device settings)
  • Request a copy of your purchase history

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
